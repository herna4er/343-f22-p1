Welcome to the unofficial FC Barcelona fan club!

For any new fans out there, this is the club's beautiful stadium, the Spotify Camp Nou. The Camp Nou has been the home of our beloved club since 1957.

"Mes Que Un Club" - This is a moto that is well know by every Barca fan. The phrase translates to "more than a club" and has been the club's moto ever since the early 20th century.
